MSOL Data Governance Communication
Driving Adoption Through Structured Engagement
A strategic communication framework to align roles, responsibilities, and escalation paths for effective data governance at MSOL.

Key Takeaways

  • Structured Cadence: Regular meetings ensure alignment and timely escalation.
  • Defined Escalation Path: Clear hierarchy from Data Admins to Executive Sponsors.
  • Role Clarity: Each role has defined communication responsibilities and channels.
  • Change Champions: All roles promote data governance within their domains.

Communication Flow

Executive Sponsors

Set vision, communicate strategy, receive escalations from Council.

Data Governance Council

Translate strategy into policy, communicate with Domain Execs.

Data Domain Executives

Bridge Council and operational levels, guide Data Owners.

Data Owners

Communicate policies, guide Stewards, report data quality.

Data Stewards

Coordinate daily data management, escalate persistent issues.

Data Administrators

Provide operational updates, alert Stewards of issues.

Meeting Cadences

  • Executive Sponsors ↔ Council: Semi-annual reviews
  • Council ↔ Domain Execs: Quarterly strategy sessions
  • Domain Execs ↔ Owners: Monthly/Bi-monthly meetings
  • Owners ↔ Stewards: Monthly data quality reviews
  • Stewards ↔ Admins: Ongoing collaboration
